About Kamal Haasan

Kamal Haasan is a renowned Indian actor, director, producer, and screenwriter. With a career spanning over six decades, he has acted in more than 200 films in Tamil, Telugu, Hindi, Malayalam, and Kannada. Known for his versatility and dedication to his craft, Kamal Haasan has become a legend in Indian cinema.

Career Highlights

Kamal Haasan's career is marked by numerous iconic roles and critically acclaimed performances. Some of his notable films include:

  • "Nayakan" (1987)
  • "Indian" (1996)
  • "Hey Ram" (2000)
  • "Vishwaroopam" (2013)
  • "Dasavathaaram" (2008)

Achievements

Kamal Haasan has received numerous awards and accolades for his contributions to cinema, including:

  • Padma Shri (1990)
  • Padma Bhushan (2014)
  • National Film Awards for Best Actor (4 times)
  • Filmfare Awards South
